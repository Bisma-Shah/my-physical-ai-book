# Learning Outcomes

By the end of this course, students will:
- Understand principles of Physical AI and embodied intelligence
- Master ROS 2 for robotic control
- Simulate robots using Gazebo and NVIDIA Isaac
- Develop humanoid robots for natural interactions
- Integrate GPT models for conversational robotics
- Complete a capstone autonomous humanoid project
